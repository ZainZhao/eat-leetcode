## 1164 指定日期的产品价格

```sql
写一段 SQL来查找在 2019-08-16 时全部产品的价格，假设所有产品在修改前的价格都是 10。

查询结果格式如下例所示：
Products table:
+------------+-----------+-------------+
| product_id | new_price | change_date |
+------------+-----------+-------------+
| 1          | 20        | 2019-08-14  |
| 2          | 50        | 2019-08-14  |
| 1          | 30        | 2019-08-15  |
| 1          | 35        | 2019-08-16  |
| 2          | 65        | 2019-08-17  |
| 3          | 20        | 2019-08-18  |
+------------+-----------+-------------+
Result table:
+------------+-------+
| product_id | price |
+------------+-------+
| 2          | 50    |
| 1          | 35    |
| 3          | 10    |
+------------+-------+
```

```sql
(select product_id as product_id,10 as price 
from Products 
group by product_id
having min(change_date) > '2019-08-16')   # 2019-08-16之前没改过的
union
(select product_id,new_price as price 
from Products 
where (product_id, change_date) in
                                (select product_id, max(change_date) as max_date  # 2019-08-16之前最新的记录
                                 from Products
                                 where change_date <= '2019-08-16'
                                 group by product_id))
```
